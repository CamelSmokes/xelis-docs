# Home

Welcome to the XELIS documentation!

This simple guide is designed to help developers and users understand the XELIS platform.

Inside, you'll find detailed information on the core features and functionalities of the XELIS Blockchain,
including its Proof-of-Work consensus algorithm, Smart Contracts capabilities, and privacy measures.

You'll also learn how to set up and configure your own XELIS node, interact with the blockchain APIs and other tools to easily use or integrate XELIS.

Whether you're an experienced blockchain developer or just getting started, this documentation is the official resource for working with XELIS ecosystem.

Dive in and discover the permissionless decentralized technology of XELIS !
